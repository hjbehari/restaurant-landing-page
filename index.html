The CLASS Framework

Five pillars. One whole-student support system.

This section should be colorful, scannable, and ad-friendly because it naturally includes the keywords Google needs: career readiness, leadership, academic support, service, and mentorship.

C

Career Readiness

Communication, teamwork, time management, financial literacy, career pathways, resumes, and interviews.

L

Leadership

Student voice, youth advisory roles, workshops, retreats, goal-setting, civic engagement, and leadership portfolios.

A

Academics

Study skills, tutoring, academic coaching, test prep, college and scholarship support, and learning habits.

S

Service

Student-led service projects, local partnerships, empathy-building, community contribution, and youth showcases.

S

Support

Peer mentorship, adult mentorship, family engagement, wellness activities, and transition support.
